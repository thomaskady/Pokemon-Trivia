Welcome, trainer! We have created this simulated environment to teach you about type advantages and Pokemon species before you embark on your own journey. There are hundreds of species to memorize, with a wide variety of typings and traits to understand.

To catch more Pokemon, you must fulfill two requirements. First, you must send out a Pokemon that has a type advantage over the wild Pokemon. Second, you must be able to identify the Pokemon species. Unfortunately your Pokedex can only provide a scrambled version of the species name until you capture it. Correctly unscramble the name to complete the capture. Once you have captured the Pokemon, it will be stored in your PC and you may use it in future battles.

To begin, I have provided you with 3 of your very own representative Pokemon. Below, you will find the Grass Pokemon BULBASAUR, the Fire Pokemon CHARMANDER, and the Water Pokemon SQUIRTLE. If you forget their typings, you can always check your Pokedex.

These Pokemon will not provide the type coverage you need for every encounter. Part of being a good trainer is knowing what fights you can take. If you do not have a Pokemon representative available to you that can inflict a super effective attack, you will have to flee. As you capture more Pokemon, you will be able to inflict more super effective attacks and capture more Pokemon.

It's time for you to encounter some Pokemon. You will find that some species are more common than others, so don't get discouraged if it takes some time to complete your Pokedex. Whenever you're ready, click "Next Encounter" below.
